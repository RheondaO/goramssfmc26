Data: Schema & Integrity

The foundation of stability. This page showcases the database design and relational schemas required to house student financial profiles. To maintain strict data privacy, we utilize a custom Python engine to generate high-fidelity "Dummy Data" for testing and validation.

Compliance & Privacy Disclaimer: All data displayed in this showcase is synthetic and purposefully anonymized to demonstrate system logic and architectural integrity while adhering to FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act) standards and VCU Information Security best practices.
